The Local Volume Complete Cluster Survey

LoVoCCS is an unprecedented cluster weak-lensing survey, creating 2D mass maps for over 100 local systems.

We are contributing to cosmology and astrophysics research, and are a precursor project for the Legacy Survey of Space and Time (LSST)!
